# DSTA Reference — Final Project Context

Source: https://www.dsta.gov.sg/ (Who We Are, What We Do pages)
Date captured: 2026-06-18

## Who They Are

**DSTA** — Defence Science and Technology Agency (Singapore)
- Leading technology organisation driving innovation for Singapore's defence
- Delivers state-of-the-art capabilities to the **Singapore Armed Forces (SAF)** 
- Harnesses science and technology — engineers and IT professionals provide multidisciplinary expertise
- Also contributes technological expertise to **national-level developments** beyond defence

**Mission:** Harness and exploit science and technology, and provide technological and engineering support, to meet the defence and national security needs of Singapore.

**Vision:** Inspired people, bringing innovation to all we connect.

**Values:** Integrity, Professionalism, Excellence, Respect, Teamwork

**Risk Appetite:**
- Low operational risk appetite related to business continuity, safety, and delivery of capabilities
- Higher risk tolerance when pushing the envelope with innovation ideas and new technologies for groundbreaking defence capabilities
- Employs sound enterprise risk management, transparent decision-making, effective communication

**Structure:** Programme Centres as principal line operating units (responsible for programme delivery and system effectiveness)

## What They Do — Technical Domains

1. **Systems Engineering** — Core competency, managing complex defence systems lifecycle
2. **Digitalised Platforms** — Fully digitised military vehicles, smart digital technologies
3. **Cyber** — Cyber AI, protecting military systems, women in cyber initiatives
4. **Software Development** — Custom defence applications
5. **Land Systems** — Hunter Armoured Fighting Vehicle (Army's first fully digitised vehicle)
6. **Naval Systems** — Unmanned surface vessels, mine detection/neutralisation
7. **Air Systems** — Advanced air defence solutions
8. **C3 Development** (Command, Control, Communication) — Situational awareness platforms
9. **Simulation & Training Systems** — Immersive training solutions
10. **Data Science & ML** — Visualisation and machine learning models for defence
11. **AI Engineering** — NLP for defence operations, human language understanding
12. **Cloud Technology** — Defence applications on cloud infrastructure
13. **InfoComm Infrastructure** — Communication infrastructure for defence
14. **Systems Resiliency** — Ensuring defence systems remain operational under stress
15. **Sustainability** — Green building solutions for defence infrastructure
16. **Information Systems** — Ops-tech partnership with military personnel
17. **Enterprise IT** — Digital solutions across the organisation
18. **Procurement & Corporate** — Cost-effective procurement for defence capabilities

## Notable Projects & Systems

- **Hunter Armoured Fighting Vehicle** — Army's first fully digitised vehicle
- **Unmanned Surface Vessels** — Naval mine detection and neutralisation
- **All-in-one C3 platform** for situational awareness
- **Sustainable building solutions** for defence infrastructure
